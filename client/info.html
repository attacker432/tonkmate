1. Commands:
    Guest commands:
  • /serverstats shows a list of server stats!
  • /help shows a list of commands!
  • /km | /killme kills your own tank!
  • /developer [code] claim developer code and access featured tanks!
    • Member commands:
  • /bc boardcast an announcement!
    • Trusted member commands:
  • /mute [id] mute a player!
  • /unmute [id] unmute a player!
    • Ambassador commands:
  • /kickdead kicks dead players!
    • Moderator commands:
  • /kick [id] kick a player!
  • /mapsize [1000-8000] sets the map size!
  • /ban [id] bans a player!
    • Admin commands:
  • /killspecs kills basic(basic tank) tanks!
  • /restart restarts the server!
  • /loadnextmaze loads the next maze!
  • /addtoken [token] adds a token!
  • /removetoken [token] removes a token!
  • /regenon enable regeneration!
  • /regenoff disable regeneration!
  • /size [value] you can change the tank size!
    • Owner commands:
  • /botscount [0-40] sets how much bots to be in the server!
  • /recoilon enable recoil!
  • /recoiloff disable recoil!
  • /aion enable auto-turrets!
  • /aioff disable auto-turrets!
  • /food [value] change the max food!
  • /killall kills all entities!
    • Developer commands:
  • /kill [id] kills a player!
  • /closeArena [1-5] spawns arena closers to close the arena!
2. Rules:
  • Teaming is allowed but when taken to extremes you can be punished!
  • Abusing fantasty tanks is allowed but when taken to extremes you can be punished!
  • Swearing/bad words is/are not allowed resulting in a ban!
  • Abusing commands its not fun for others and this will result in a punish and a ban!
